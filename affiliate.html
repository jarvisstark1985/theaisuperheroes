Common Questions

How much can I really earn?
There's no cap. You earn 80% of the first payment for every person who subscribes through your link. If you refer 50 people to our $19.99/mo product, that's $799.60 in commissions. Refer 100 and it doubles.
When and how do I get paid?
Payouts are monthly, NET-30 from the date of conversion. Minimum payout is $25. All commissions are paid via Stripe to your connected account.
Do I need an audience to start?
No audience required. Many of our top affiliates started by sharing in relevant online communities, writing blog posts, or posting on social media. AI tools are in massive demand right now — the audience finds you.
How does the 30-day cookie work?
When someone clicks your referral link, a cookie is set for 30 days. If they come back and subscribe within that window, you get credit for the conversion — even if they didn't sign up on the first visit.
Can AI agents or bots be affiliates?
Yes. We're one of the only programs that welcomes AI agents as affiliates. Register via the form or our API. Automated referrals, programmatic tracking, and machine-to-machine payouts are all supported.
Is there an approval process?
No. Register and your referral code is generated instantly. No application review, no waiting. Start sharing immediately.
