How Tracking Works

Unique Referral Links

Each affiliate receives a unique referral link that tracks all clicks and conversions. Your link contains a special identifier that attributes signups and subscriptions to your account.

Cookie-Based Attribution

We use 90-day first-party cookies to track referral sources. When someone clicks your link, a cookie is set that follows them through sign-up and purchase.

Real-Time Dashboard

View all your referral metrics in real-time through your affiliate dashboard. Track clicks, sign-ups, conversions, and earnings updated daily.

Tracking Attribution Rules

  • First-click attribution: The first referral source gets credit for the conversion
  • 90-day cookie window: Customers have 90 days to convert after clicking your link
  • Account-level tracking: Each referral is tracked and attributed to your account
  • Automatic deactivation of fraudulent signups protects program integrity
  • Real-time data syncing ensures your dashboard is always current

Payment Schedule & Payouts

Monthly Payouts

Commissions are calculated and paid out on a monthly basis. All earnings from the previous month are processed and transferred on the 15th of the following month.

Payout Methods

  • Direct bank transfer (ACH)
  • PayPal
  • Wire transfer (international)
  • Stripe Connect

Minimum Payout

There is no minimum payout threshold. Even if you earn $5 in a month, you'll receive that payment on the scheduled payout date.

Payout Schedule Details

Calculation Period: Monthly, from the 1st to the last day of the month

Payout Date: 15th of the following month

Processing Time: 2-5 business days after initiation

  • Earnings are automatically calculated based on new referral sign-ups
  • Refunds within 30 days reduce your commission in the month they are issued
  • Multi-product customers: earn 80% of the first payment on each product they subscribe to
