Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn commissions? ▼
Share your unique referral link with your audience. When someone clicks your link and subscribes to any of our four products, you'll earn 80% of their first payment—one time per referral.
Is the 80% commission one-time or recurring? ▼
It's one-time per referral. You earn 80% of the first payment when someone subscribes through your link. However, there's no limit on how many people you can refer—earn unlimited bonuses by referring unlimited customers.
When and how do I get paid? ▼
Commissions are calculated monthly and paid out on the 15th of each month via direct transfer. Track earnings in real-time through your referral dashboard.
Can I earn on multiple products per customer? ▼
Absolutely! If a customer you referred subscribes to multiple products, you earn 80% of the first payment on each product they purchase.
Is there a limit to how much I can earn? ▼
No limits! The more referrals you bring, the more you earn. Your earning potential is unlimited.
How do you track referrals? ▼
Each referral link is unique to you and tracked via secure cookies and server-side attribution. When someone uses your link to sign up, they're permanently attributed to you.
What if I have more questions? ▼
Email us at support@theaisuperheroes.com or check out our detailed Referral Program Details page.
